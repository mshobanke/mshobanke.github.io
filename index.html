Correlation Between Book Ratings and Customer Reviews

In this project, an end-to-end analysis of the relationship between book ratings and customer reviews is performed. I built a graph database for storing extracted data from its source, developed a Machine Learning and Natural Language Processing pipeline to identify non-meaningful reviews - aiding a more accurate statistical analysis of the correlation between ratings and review.

Framingham Study: Prediction of Heart Disease

Here, we utilize Logistics Regression to predict the risk of heart disease using factors that affect cardiovascular health. In a bid to improve prediction accuracy, the logistics regression algorithm is written from scratch in comparison to inbuilt python library, prioritizing optimization of the derived relationhip (equation) over latency. Additionally, the dependency between each factor and risk of heart disease is examined, leading to the exploration of bias in the dataset and possibly stratifying the data to improve result accuracy!
